About Us

This application is built to allow community health workers and public health officers to carry out their roles more effectively. It ensures a better communitcation channel between registered community health institutions and members of the community.

The platform also serves to inform its user through the blog/news feature which focuses on healthcare and healthy living.

Vision

To create community-centric, self-sustaining digital healthcare services.

Mission

To create a community model for improving access and delivery of healthcare services according to society needs.
